How it works

Operational automation, not theater

SignalFoundry treats SOC automation as a controlled system: it ingests alerts, applies policy and known-FP logic, enforces redaction and reconciliation gates, and routes output into evidence packs or review lanes.

Ingest Wazuh-backed telemetry enters a deterministic poll and triage flow rather than a hand-curated demo loop.
Triage Policy, rule overrides, and known-FP handling suppress repetitive noise while keeping higher-risk cases visible.
Quality gates Coverage, freshness, redaction, and reconciliation keep the public proof path tied to verified operational state.
Package and route Review-ready summaries and escalation artifacts preserve analyst context instead of dropping raw alerts on a queue.
Workflow movement Verified pipeline

The system keeps deterministic control visible: ingest, triage, redact, reconcile, coverage check, and package.
